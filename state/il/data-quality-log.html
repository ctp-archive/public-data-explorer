November 9, 2020

Variables affected:

  • negative

Tags: CTP Backfill CTP Backfill Mistake Correction

On November 9, 2020, Data Entry subtracted an un-updated confirmed number from the Total PCR tests (specimens) number to calculate negatives. Since IL removed this metric on November 6, 2020, we should use the Cases (confirmed and probable) number in the calculation instead. On November 10,2020, we backfilled this mistake.

November 6, 2020

Variables affected:

  • positiveCasesViral
  • probableCases

On November 6, 2020, Illinois stopped reporting Confirmed and Probable case numbers separately. Due to this we cannot continue reporting these metrics, but the time series for them is available in the historical data and in our data downloads.

October 31, 2020

Variables affected:

  • totalTestsViral
  • positiveTestsViral
  • positiveCasesViral
  • positive
  • negative
  • death
  • deathConfirmed

On October 31, 2020, Illinois did not update their dashboard during our daily publication shift, but they did release their numbers in a (press release)[http://www.dph.illinois.gov/news/public-health-officials-announce-7899-new-confirmed-cases-coronavirus-disease]. We used this alternative source to update Total PCR tests (specimens), Positive PCR tests (people), Cases (confirmed plus probable), Negative PCR tests (people), Deaths (confirmed and probable), and Deaths (confirmed).

October 31, 2020

Variables affected:

  • totalTestsViral
  • positiveCasesViral
  • positive
  • negative
  • death
  • deathConfirmed
  • probableCases

On October 31, 2020, IL did not update update anything but hospitalization metrics on their dashboard. We updated tests, cases, and deaths with (this press release) [http://www.dph.illinois.gov/news/public-health-officials-announce-7899-new-confirmed-cases-coronavirus-disease].

June 8, 2020 to July 27, 2020

Variables affected:

  • probableCases

Tags: CTP Backfill New Metric

CTP didn't have a column for probables until 7/28 and we started a confirmed cases column on 4/29. IL didn't start separating out confirmed and probables until 6/8. Calculated probables as positive - positive(PCR) from 6/9 to 7/28.

May 25, 2020 to June 6, 2020
