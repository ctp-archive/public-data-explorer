August 24, 2020

Question

How do you de-dupe?

Answer

August 24, 2020

Answered by

We de-duplicate cases by automated and manual means. Using the excel sheet as an example: in Acadia Parish on March 16, there were 3 positive tests but there was only 1 case. Even if that 1 case accounted all of the positive tests the next day, that 1 person wouldn't be counted again as a case. On the next day when there is another case, that is a different person.
