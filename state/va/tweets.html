February 28, 2021, 4:01 PM PST

States use different methods for accounting for COVID-19 deaths. Virginia has used a slower technique, which has resulted in a huge spike in reported deaths now, more than a month after the cases peaked in the state. VA has reported 1454 deaths over the last 9 days. https://t.co/uEg4kNmfwX

March 1, 2021, 4:21 PM PST

As we noted yesterday, VA has been reconciling death certificates for the last 9 days which resulted in nearly 1,500 additional deaths. That number had a noticeable impact on national figures. Here's a look at the 7-day day average excluding VA. https://t.co/dD8gOhaFeE

March 3, 2021, 4:15 PM PST

VA is still reconciling death certificates from the post holiday surge. As a result, their death count is artificially inflated. Nationally, deaths are still declining. https://t.co/gv1xPxFwcC
