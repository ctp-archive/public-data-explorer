April 8, 2021, 10:21 AM PDT

Notes

NE does not report COVID-19 data related to long-term-care facilities online. Long-term-care reporting has occurred in press conferences. The last known update was June 15, 2020. After June 15, 2020, CTP only received updated information via email from state officials. Updates were infrequent, resulting in weeks of data being carried over. May 12, 2020: Governor Ricketts gave cumulative totals for long-term care cases and deaths at a press conference. This data is reflected in the totals on May 21, 2020. May 28, 2020: Governor Ricketts gave cumulative totals for long-term care cases and deaths at a press conference. June 11, 2020: Dr. Gary Anthone, director of Public Health for DHHS, gave cumulative totals for long-term care cases and deaths in a press conference. June 15, 2020: Dr. Gary Anthone, director of Public Health for DHHS, gave cumulative totals for long-term care cases and deaths in a press conference. June 18, 2020: No update for long-term care was given. Data from the June 15, 2020 press conference was carried over to August 8, 2020. August 8, 2020: Nebraska health officials sent CTP a spreadsheet with long-term care data. Data from this August 8, 2020 spreadsheet was reported for August 13, 2020. September 24, 2020: Nebraska health officials sent CTP a spreadsheet with long-term care data. October 1, 2020: Nebraska health officials did not send CTP a spreadsheet. Data is carried over from September 24, 2020. October 29, 2020: Cumulative deaths went down by 2. Outbreak deaths went up by 31. November 26th, 2020: Nebraska health officials did not send CTP a spreadsheet. Data is carried over from November 19th, 2020. May 28, 2020: Governor Ricketts gave cumulative totals for long-term care cases and deaths at a press conference. June 11, 2020: Dr. Gary Anthone, director of Public Health for DHHS, gave cumulative totals for long-term care cases and deaths in a press conference. June 15, 2020: Dr. Gary Anthone, director of Public Health for DHHS, gave cumulative totals for long-term care cases and deaths in a press conference. June 18, 2020: No update for long-term care was given. Data from the June 15, 2020 press conference was carried over to August 8, 2020. August 8, 2020: Nebraska health officials sent CTP a spreadsheet with long-term care data. Data from this August 8, 2020 spreadsheet was reported for August 13, 2020. September 24, 2020: Nebraska health officials sent CTP a spreadsheet with long-term care data. October 1, 2020: Nebraska health officials did not send CTP a spreadsheet. Data is carried over from September 24, 2020. October 29, 2020: Cumulative deaths went down by 2. Outbreak deaths went up by 31.
