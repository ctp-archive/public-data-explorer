July 9, 2020 to July 22, 2020

Variables affected:

  • negative

Tags: CTP Backfill CTP Backfill Mistake Correction

On July 9, 2020 the Northern Mariana Islands began reporting Total PCR tests (people), and we should have begun using Total PCR tests (people) - Confirmed cases to calculate Negatives. However this didn't become standard until July 23, 2020. Patching the incorrect values in Negative with Total PCR tests (people) - Confirmed cases. This update will cause Negatives to decrease on July 9, 2020 due to the change in calculation.

March 28, 2020 to April 1, 2020

Variables affected:

  • death

Tags: CTP Backfill

We missed recording Total deaths for the Northern Mariana Islands on March 28, 2020 to April 1, 2020. According to a query on the Northern Mariana Islands ArcGis dashboard the first reported death was March 31, 2020. Carrying over the zeroes to March 30, 2020 and then carrying the 1.
