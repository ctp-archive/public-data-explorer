Variables affected:

  • totalTestsViral
  • totalTestsPeopleViral

Alabama documents on its dashboard that the state's totals are unique people, but we had them stored in specimens because of (old) conflicting evidence received via outreach. We recently found social media posts from ADPH verifying that unique people was the correct interpretation. We are going to switch the state's timeseries to unique people. We will also switch totalTestResults to draw from unique people since it was previously drawing from specimens.
