April 8, 2021, 10:21 AM PDT

Notes

Wyoming reports long-term care data for Nursing Homes and Assisted Living Facilities. Wyoming reports cumulative cases and deaths. This data is cumulative since March, 13 2020. Wyoming Department of Health releases data to CTP via email. Wyoming provides facility-level data for nursing homes, assisted living facilities and rehabilitation centers. According to the Wyoming Department of Health, "numbers of deaths listed in this report for each facility should be considered provisional; not every death listed has been confirmed through death certificate review for announcement by WDH or inclusion in the official WDH count of COVID-19 related deaths." On July 2, 2020: CTP began receiving updated information from Wyoming’s Public Information Department. On August 27, 2020, Wyoming reported that one resident death is not included in Wyoming's total death count due to the resident having permanent residency in Montana. CTP includes this fatality in Wyoming's long-term care data. October 22, 2020: Two Nursing Home facilities in Wyoming staff cases decreased. Douglas Care Center staff cases dropped from 7 to 2. New Horizons Care Center staff cases dropped from 4 to 2. November 5, 2020: Due to technical difficulties, Wyoming was unable to release data this week. Data was carried over from previous week November 26, 2020: Wyoming did not release data this week. Data was carried over from previous week. December 24, 2020: Wyoming did not provide updated data to CTP. Data was carried over from previous week and outbreak data removed. January 7, 2021: Assisted living staff cases decreased from 56 to 35, and probably staff cases from 33 to 24. January 14, 2021: Wyoming did not provide CTP with updated LTC data. Carried over total from January 7, 2021. January 28, 2021: Wyoming did not provide updated data to CTP. Data was carried over from previous week and outbreak data removed. February 4, 2021: Wyoming began emailing CTP biweekly with information. On the off weeks, data will be carried from the previous reported week.
