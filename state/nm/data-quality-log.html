April 28, 2020 to December 26, 2020

Variables affected:

  • positiveCasesViral

Tags: State Annotation / Source Change

We used to store New Mexico cases in the Positive Cases (PCR) field because New Mexico calls them "Positive test results" on their website. However, there is no indication anywhere on the website, nor in our outreach to the state, that clarifies that these positives are PCR only. Moreover, reports such as KHN's have implied that New Mexico includes individuals with positive antigen tests in the figure. On December 27, 2020 we are clearing the Positive Cases (PCR) history to reflect that there is no evidence for this classification.
