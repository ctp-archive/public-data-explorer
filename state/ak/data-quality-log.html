March 6, 2020 to November 15, 2020

Variables affected:

  • hospitalizedCumulative
  • positiveCasesViral
  • positive

Tags: CTP Backfill CTP API / Logic Changes New Metric

On November 16, 2020, we backfilled Cumulative hospitalized from March 6, 2020 through November 15, 2020, and we are beginning capture today. Additionally, we removed non-residents from our Cases (confirmed and probable) metric to comply with federal case reporting guidelines and to match our policy in Florida. We adjusted our Negative PCR tests (people or cases) column accordingly. Previously, we copied the positive values from Confirmed cases to Cases (confirmed and probable), but since Alaska's cases value lumps probable cases with confirmed cases, we zeroed out the Confirmed cases time series.
