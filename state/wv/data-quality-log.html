March 21, 2020 to April 15, 2020

Variables affected:

  • inIcuCumulative
  • onVentilatorCumulative
  • hospitalizedCumulative

Tags: CTP Backfill

We recorded Ever hospitalized, Even in ICU, and Ever on ventilator for West Virginia briefly between March 21, 2020 and April 15, 2020, however the source of these values was determined to not fully represent cumulative totals for these metrics. Removing values recorded in Ever hospitalized, Even in ICU, and Ever on ventilator.

March 13, 2020 to March 5, 2021

Variables affected:

  • totalTestResults

Tags: CTP API / Logic Changes

As of March 5, 2021, West Virginia's totalTestResults are drawn directly from totalTestsViral instead of calculated via positive+negative. This change resulted in a decrease of 26,637 total tests due to the removal of probables from the totals.

March 6, 2020 to March 4, 2021

Variables affected:

  • negative

Tags: CTP Backfill

On March 5, 2021, we cleared the history of West Virginia's Negative (people or cases). The number previously mixed units and is no longer needed since totalTestResults are no longer calculated via positive+negative.

March 13, 2020 to March 3, 2021

Variables affected:

  • totalTestsViral

Tags: CTP Backfill

On March 5, 2021, we backfilled the history of West Virginia's Total PCR Tests (specimens) from data scraped from its dashboard. This allowed us to get historical testing data we did not have from March 13, 2020 to April 28, 2020.

March 2, 2021

Variables affected:

  • negative
  • positive
  • positiveCasesViral
  • probableCases

Tags: State Anomalies

On March 2, 2021, the West Virginia Department of Health and Human Resources [announced](https://dhhr.wv.gov/News/2021/Pages/COVID-19-Daily-Update-3-2-2021.aspx) that a backlog of testing results from December and January had been reported to the state after the February 26, 2021 dashboard update was published. These testing results were mistakenly reported as positive instead of negative, and are being corrected in the electronic disease surveillance system. This issue is expected to be resolved on the dashboard within the next few days. We urge caution when interpreting numbers from this time period.
